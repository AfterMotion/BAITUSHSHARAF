Baitush Sharaf is an exceptional organization in the history of the practice and usage of spirituality. The main goal and purpose of the organization is the pure practice of Tasawwuf by assimilating the entire Islamic way of life. Since its inception, this organization working for the moral, social and spiritual purity of the people. Baitush Sharaf is a place of refuge, service, kindness, love and a centre for the practice and research of science. The great founder of this organization was Qutbul Alam Shah Sufi Hazrat Maulana Mir Mohammad Akhtar (Rah.). With his extraordinary wisdom, unparalleled energy, personality, intelligence and good character, he earned the respect and love of the people, including contemporary scholars.

Hazrat Shah Sufi Maulana Mir Muhammad Akhter (Rah.)

Qutbul Alam Shah Sufi Hazrat Maulana Mir Mohammad Akhtar (Rah.), the great founder of Baitush Sharaf, especially felt the need to establish a constitutional organization to promote the faith and spiritual upliftment of the servants of Allah and to help in worldly needs. For this purpose, he founded a well-known organization called Anjuman-e Ittehad Bangladesh on 2 October 1952. Today, the establishment and operation of hundreds of mosques, madrasahs, orphanages, eye, cripple and children's hospitals in the country and abroad, the organization of various faith-spiritual gatherings and the implementation of service activities are the actual fruits of the sincere realization of that great spiritual saint. Before his death, he entrusted the responsibility of the caliphate in front of the Holy Kaaba to his worthy caliph Hadiye Zaman Shah Sufi Hazrat Maulana Mohammad Abdul Jabbar - Huzur Kebla (Rah.). Hazrat Shah Sufi Maulana Mohammad Abdul Jabbar (Rah.)

Hazrat Shah Sufi Maulana Muhammad Abdul Jabbar (Rah.) has been the President of Anjuman-e Ittehad Bangladesh since 1980. Following the footsteps of his Pir-Murshids, this great saint relentlessly devoted himself to the propagation of the Shari'ah and Tariqat. In fulfilling the responsibilities entrusted to him by Murshid-e Barhak, he devoted his whole strength and life to the purpose of formulating, adopting and implementing various service-oriented plans. He spread various mahfils and charitable organizations all over Bangladesh for the nafs and chafaye kulub in Tajkabiya through Tarikaye Aliaye Kaderiya. He established the branches of Anjuman-e Ittehad Bangladesh at home and abroad. He also founded 60 mosques, 15 orphanages, 10 madrasahs and hifzkhanas, research institutes, charitable clinics, hospitals throughout the country. The actual outcome of his education movement was the International Islamic University Chittagong. The introduction of an interest-free banking system was the country's largest financial institution at the private level, Islami Bank Bangladesh Limited. Read More...

Hazrat Shah Sufi Maulana Mohammad Kutub Uddin (Rah.)

Hazrat Shah Sufi Maulana Mohammad Qutub Uddin (Rah.) was appointed as the President of Anjuman-e Ittehad Bangladesh from April 1998. Under his dynamic leadership, Anjuman-e Ittehad Bangladesh became a religious, social and cultural movement. He continued his efforts to carry forward the programs left by Huzur Kebla Allama Shah Abdul Jabbar (Rah.). During his tenure of 22 years, many new mosques, madrasahs, orphanages and hifzkhanas have been established all over the country. He passed away on 20 May 2020, at the age of Holy Shab-e Qadr. According to his last wish, he was buried next to his Peer Murshid. Read More...

Allama Mohammad Abdul Hai Nadwi (M.J.A)

Allama Mohammad Abdul Hai Nadawi (M.J.A), the notable son of Allama Shah Abdul Jabbar (Rah.), a prominent Islamic Thinker, Teacher, Researcher and Author of many books, has taken on this great responsibility as the 4th Peer Saheb of Baitush Sharaf, after the passing of the above three saints. As the President of Anjuman-e Ittehad Bangladesh, he has dedicated himself to its development, expansion, reputation and prosperity. It's our firm belief that under his leadership, like his father, who is seated in a seat of high dignity and respect, Baitush Sharaf will spread its fame not only in the homeland but also in the world.
